Spicy Plum Marinade

Author: Martin Pakandl

Do you love meat combined with sweet sauces? Then you have to try the marinade from plum and orange marmalade. There is also chili pepper in it, which gives it a pleasent taste tip. Start grilling ang impress your friends with new flavors.

  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • Difficulty: easy

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 12 pieces of plum or jam
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 red chili pepper
  • 1/2 cup of orange marmalade

Procedure

1 If we have fresth plums, finely chop them

2Crush the pepper.

3Put everything in a pot, add orange juice and marmalade and cook slowly for about half an hour.

4Brush the meat with the marinade while griling.
